A student's education should not be limited by a lack of resources.
The current Indian education system is fully reliant on the costly and unequal tuition system, leaving families struggling to pay for the education of their children, and kids left without the resources they need to succeed. It's time for a change.
The Problem
In India, the state of education is a dire one. The quality of education in a majority of schools has diminished, forcing students to seek out other avenues for education. Most turn to tuition, a multi billion dollar industry filling the void that sub-par schools have left behind. However, these tuitions are very costly as the students are in need of help, and no one seems to be trying to provide quality free resources because of the potential revenue. This leaves students with whatever quality of tuition they can afford, and beyond that not much else.
Our Mission
At Matli, we believe no one's education should be stifled by a lack of resources. When we learned about the lack of educational resources and the high cost associated with the tuition system, we were determined there must be a better way. Through our platform, we plan to provide students with a high quality, no cost resource that enables them to succeed in their educational pursuits. Our platform will be specifically tailored to the Central Board of Secondary Education curriculum to help students excel in their X Standard and XII Standard Board Exams.
